Chronic Conditions

It is commonly believed that chronic conditions tend to be age-related and aff mostly the elderly. However, many of them are "getting younger" by the day: type 2 diabetes mellitus, musculoskeletal disorders (joint, ligament and muscular conditions), atherosclerosis and other various diseases are more often than not diagnosed in people under the age of 25 years. Chronic conditions are difficult to treat, and typically the best thing doctors can do is reverse symptoms and prevent the disease from progressing. Nevertheless, at Swiss Medica we find that chronic conditions do respond to treatment and that is why we've made this issue our priority.

Chronic Conditions

The second key focus of our activity is treatments aimed at the prevention of aging. Our anti-age treatments provide internal rejuvenation and include various cosmetic procedures for a youthful appearance. Look and feel younger and more energized!
